# Problem Statement: Age & Gender Prediction from Face Images

## Competition
**Platform:** Kaggle  
**Start:** Nov 7, 2025 | **Close:** Nov 15, 2025

## Overview

The goal of this competition was to **predict a person's age and gender from a face image**.

### Given
- A **training dataset** of face images with corresponding labels:
  - `age` — a numeric value
  - `gender` — binary (0 or 1)
- A **test dataset** of face images without labels

### Task
Build a model that takes a face image as input and outputs:
- A predicted **age** (regression)
- A predicted **gender** (binary classification)

### Submission Format
Submit a `submission.csv` containing one row per test image with columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `id` | Unique row identifier (provided in test set) |
| `age` | Predicted age (numeric, continuous) |
| `gender` | Predicted gender (0 or 1) |

---

## Objective

Develop a **PyTorch model** that predicts both:
- **Gender** — binary classification
- **Age** — regression

from facial images. Design, train, and evaluate models effectively using two approaches:
1. **Simple CNN from scratch**
2. **Fine-tuned CNN from pretrained models**

## Evaluation Metric

**Harmonic Mean of F1 Score and nRMSE (Higher is Better)**

The competition evaluates two targets:

| Target | Type | Metric |
|--------|------|--------|
| `age` | Regression | RMSE (clamped and normalized) |
| `gender` | Classification | Macro F1 |

### Score Formulas

**1. Age Score (nRMSE, normalized & clamped):**

$$\text{age score} = 1 - \frac{\text{clamp}(\text{RMSE}(\hat{y}_{\text{age}}, y_{\text{age}}), 30)}{30}$$

**2. Gender Score:**

$$\text{gender score} = F_{1,\text{macro}}$$

**3. Final Score (Harmonic Mean — higher is better):**

$$\text{final score} = \frac{2 \cdot (\text{gender score} \cdot \text{age score})}{\text{gender score} + \text{age score}}$$
